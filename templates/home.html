Introducing The Zuhause Project, a 501(c)(3)-backed project to unite the people of Vienna in the fight for a housing & city development approach that encourages integration, reduces the wealth gap, and promotes socioeconomic equality.

The Zuhause Platform platform proposes research-backed solutions to housing & development, economic modeling and social inequality data analytics, and raises awareness by connecting students to the latest journal publishings in the field.
